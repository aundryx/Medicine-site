Aloe Vera

Aloe vera has long been regarded as a natural remedy for skin and hair care. In the Philippines, it is commonly used to treat various scalp conditions such as dryness, dandruff, and itching, all of which contribute to hair loss. The plant's gel contains vitamins A, C, and E, as well as enzymes that promote better scalp health by reducing oil buildup, soothing irritation, and stimulating hair follicles. Aloe vera also has moisturizing properties that improve hydration and prevent dryness, which is often a key factor in preventing hair loss

Coconut Oil

Coconut oil is a staple in Filipino hair care, praised for its moisturizing and nourishing properties. Rich in fatty acids such as lauric acid, coconut oil penetrates deep into the hair shaft and scalp, reducing dryness and improving scalp health. This helps to strengthen hair, prevent breakage, and minimize hair loss. Studies have shown that coconut oil can also reduce protein loss in hair, making it an effective natural treatment for hair thinning and promoting overall hair strength

Calamansi

Calamansi, with its astringent and clarifying properties, is a popular ingredient in Filipino hair care routines. Its acidic nature helps to control excess oil production on the scalp, which, when left unchecked, can contribute to dandruff and hair thinning. By reducing oil buildup and maintaining a healthy pH balance, calamansi creates a clean scalp environment, developing optimal conditions for healthy hair growth. Regular use of calamansi can also help reduce dandruff, which is a common cause of hair loss
