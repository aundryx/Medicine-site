Peppermint

Peppermint has long been used for its soothing and pain-relieving properties, especially for tension headaches. Its menthol content helps relax muscles and blood circulation, which can reduce headache intensity. According to a study published in Headache: The Journal of Head and Face Pain, peppermint oil has shown efficacy in reducing headache severity when applied to the temples or inhaled

Lavender

Lavender is known for its calming effects and has been traditionally used to relieve headaches, particularly those caused by stress. A study published in Cephalalgia found that inhaling lavender oil helped reduce headache frequency and intensity, particularly tension headaches

Feverfew

Feverfew is a popular herb used to prevent and manage migraines. Research suggests that feverfew can reduce the frequency and severity of migraines due to its anti-inflammatory properties. A systematic review in The Cochrane Database of Systematic Reviews supports its use in preventing migraines and reducing headache discomfort
